Aram Daily Job Brief

2026-05-16 · 39 new pipeline additions reviewed + YC startup lane checked

Today’s scan produced volume, but only a narrow slice looks worth attention for your actual lane. The best fits continue to be roles where AI/workflow framing, customer discovery, and enterprise adoption matter more than pure quota carrying or deep engineering ownership. The cleanest current options are Glean’s AI Outcomes role, Celonis’s customer-facing applied AI consultant path, and two YC deployment/operator roles that sit close to productized AI adoption rather than generic sales.

Recommended focus

  • Prioritize AI outcomes / deployment / solutions roles that explicitly tie AI to measurable customer workflows.
  • Treat partnerships and product roles as secondary unless the JD does not quietly require deep engineering ownership or very senior ecosystem credentials.
  • Ignore flattering but low-conversion titles where architect / PM really means hands-on technical depth, cloud architecture, or heavy LLM implementation.
